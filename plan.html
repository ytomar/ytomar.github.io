Market Overview: The "$14 Billion Problem"

The Indian retail investment market is undergoing a seismic shift. While AUM has grown exponentially, a significant and persistent inefficiency has emerged: a "Value Leak" estimated at ~$14 Billion annually, representing a ~2% performance drag on portfolios. This inefficiency creates a massive market opportunity for disruptive solutions.

Projected Retail AUM Growth (India)

The retail investment market is poised for continued strong growth, driven by increasing disposable incomes and financial literacy. This growth magnifies the absolute size of the "Value Leak," making solutions more critical than ever.

Components of the "Value Leak"

The ~2% annual drag is not a single issue but a combination of factors. High hidden fees and transaction costs are significant, but consistent benchmark underperformance and flawed portfolio construction contribute heavily to the problem.
